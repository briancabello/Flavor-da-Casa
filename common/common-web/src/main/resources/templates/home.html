Good to know

Before you come in.

Do I need an account to reserve?

No. You can request a table without signing up. Just share your details when you book.

How will I know my table is confirmed?

Our team looks over each request and sends your confirmation by email.

Are you open for brunch and lunch?

Yes. We’re open throughout the day, from weekend brunch to a relaxed dinner.

Can you host groups, corporate or private dinners?

Of course. Tell us your party size when you book and we’ll set the room for you.

Can I check my reservation later?

Yes. You can check a reservation to see its status anytime.

Does the menu change?

It changes gently with the season, so there’s usually something new to come back for.
